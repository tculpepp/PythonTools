Module 4 - Background

Testing and Deployment

A project may be complete as far as the functionality in concerned, although it is not complete to the point of confidence in operation until the testing is complete. Testing may be the most overlooked phase of development, although it is probably the most important phase. Lack of proper testing prior to deployment can result is substantial costs due to system downtime, loss of data, and loss of worker productivity. Severe problems can even result in cataclysmic impact on a company and its clients. This module focuses on the testing and deployment phases of a project.
